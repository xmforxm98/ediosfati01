How to Delete Your Eidos Fati Account

You can delete your Eidos Fati account directly through the mobile app. Follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Open the Eidos Fati app on your mobile device
  2. Go to Profile/Settings (usually found in the bottom navigation or main menu)
  3. Find "Account Settings" or "Privacy Settings"
  4. Look for "Delete Account" option
  5. Confirm your password when prompted
  6. Confirm deletion by following the on-screen instructions
